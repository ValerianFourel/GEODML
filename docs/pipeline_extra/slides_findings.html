Research Question

Practical Implications for GEO

1 Structure content around questions — most reliable on-page lever. Use H2/H3 matching natural queries. Works in small candidate pools (chatbots, short lists).

2 First-party vendor content beats review sites — both LLMs demote G2/Capterra/Forbes by 1.6-5 positions. Brand pages are preferred.

3 Get mentioned in earned media — the LLM discards review sites but promotes the brands they mention. Being listed on G2/Forbes boosts your LLM rank by ~1 position. NEW

4 More earned media coverage = bigger boost — dose-response: each additional review/media page mentioning your brand adds +0.15-0.22 positions. NEW

5 Clarity over data density — LLM penalizes number-heavy pages. Clean explanatory content wins.

Two Key Insights

Insight 1: Pool size is a first-order parameter.
The same LLM produces opposite conclusions depending on how many candidates it sees. What works in a chatbot's short list may backfire in a broader AI-powered search.
Insight 2: Earned media is a sacrificial signal amplifier.
The LLM reads G2/Forbes/Capterra snippets, demotes these pages, but promotes the brands they mention. Third-party validation works — the messenger just doesn't survive.
